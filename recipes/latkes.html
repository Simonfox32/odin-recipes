Mom's Potato Latkes

Perfect shredded potatos fried in oil to make you reminisce about mom's cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups shredded potato
  • 1/4 cup grated onion
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 6 saltine crackers
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix potato, onion, eggs, crushed, crackers, in a bowl.
  2. Heat 1/4 inch oil in a heavy skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of the potato mixture, first pressing potato mixture against the side of the bowl to remove excess liquid,
    into the hot oil; slightly flatten the latkes into the oil with the back of your spoon so they are an even thickness.
  4. Cook in batches in the hot oil until browned and crisp, 3 to 5 minutes per side. Drain latkes on a paper towel-line plate.

Nutrition Facts:

Calories: 371 kcal
Fat: 30 grams
Carbs: 22 grams
Protein: 6 grams
